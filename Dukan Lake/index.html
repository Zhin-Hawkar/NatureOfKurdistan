Lake Dukan (or Lake Dokan) (Arabic:بحيرة دوكان) is a lake in Kurdistan Region. It is located close to the city of Ranya, and is a reservoir on the Little Zab created by the construction of the Dukan Dam.

The Dukan Dam was built between 1954 and 1959 as a multi-purpose dam to provide water storage, irrigation and hydroelectricity.
